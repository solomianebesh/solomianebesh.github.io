ABOUT

Mad Heads was formed in Kyiv, Ukraine. Mad Heads XL is a mix of ska, swing, and Ukrainian folk genres. Their goal has always been to give Ukrainian culture visibility. The band was one of the first bands that vocalized their support of the Orange Revolution. They cancelled their performances and played in the Kyiv Maidan Nezalezhnosti. They wrote a song specifically for the protest and it was included in the compliation “Orange Song Ukrainian Revolution.” Due to their performance being broadast over CNN and other news channels, they gained a lot of promotion. They also performed for the protestors during the 2013-14 Euro Maidan. In recent years they have been helping raise funds to support the Ukrainian army.
