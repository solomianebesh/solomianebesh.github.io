ВАРЕНИКИ

Pronounced: Vah-REH-ny-ky


As Ukrainians are inspiring the world, many are only just learning about the wonderfully rich culture and history Ukraine has. In order to stand with Ukraine I want to provide a step by step recipe for making one of Ukraine’s most delicious dishes, varenyky. Вареники are dumplings traditionally stuffed with potatoes and then boiled. They are served with sour cream and caremalized onions. My goal is to continue ot teach people about my people’s culture, what better way than through food.

TOOLS
  • Mixer
  • Bowl
  • Potato Masher
  • Large Pot
  • Rolling Pin
  • Circular Cookie Cutter
  • Pan or Tray

NOTES

  • Make the filling first and refrigerate
  • If you want GF version use GF all purpose flour
  • If you don't have a potato masher, a whisk will work
  • If you don't have a circular cookie cutter, use a glass
  • Fillings can be experimented with! Add bacon or onions!

THE DOUGH

Ingredient Measurement
Flour 4.5 Cups
Oil 2 Tablespoons
Eggs 1
Salt 2 Teaspoons
Water 1.5 Cups

THE FILLING

Ingredient Measurement
Potatoes, cubed 5
Onions, chopped 2
Butter 4 Tablespoons
Salt & Pepper To Taste
Cheddar Cheese 1 Cup

STEP 1

Cube potatoes and place into a large pot. Let the water boil until potatoes soften.

STEP 2

Drain potatoes and add into a large bowl with salt, pepper, butter and cheese. Mash until creamy texture. Put in refrigerator and let cool.

STEP 3

In a mixing bowl add flour, salt, oil, egg and water. Mix with a dough hook until you have a dough like texture.

STEP 4

Roll the dough out with a rolling pin so that it is about 1/8'' thick. Use a circular cookie cutter or glass to cut out circles.

STEP 5

Place completed dough circles onto a tray with towel and keep covered so they don't dry out.

STEP 6

Place a tablespoon of cooled potato mixture into the center of the circle.

STEP 7

Fold the dough over the potato so that the ends of the circle meet, creating a half circle shape.

STEP 8

Pinch the edges until they are sealed all the way around.

STEP 9

Place completed varenyky back onto to the tray and cover with towel.

STEP 10

Boil water and then place a few varenyky at a time. Varenyky will float on the top of the water when they are done. Remove with a spoon and place into large bowl.

STEP 11

Congratulations you made varenkyky! Serve with carmalized onions and enjoy!
