WINTER ON FIRE

October 9, 2015

Synopsis: ‘Winter on Fire: Ukraine’s Fight for Freedom’ tells the story of one such historic moment of public revolt against a near autocratic government.


In this documentary, the Euromaidan Protests of 2014 are re-told through footage of the protests and the stories of the protestors who survived. This film does a great job introducing non-Ukranians to the modern history of Russia's aggression against Ukraine.
